# The Exchange — Form Fields

## Employer Opportunity Intake Form

Form title:
The Exchange — Employer Opportunity Intake Form

Form description:
Use this form to submit a work-based learning opportunity for review by The Exchange team. Opportunities may include internships, project-based engagements, short-term roles, or other applied learning experiences. Submissions will be reviewed before being shared with partner organizations or matched with candidates.

---

## Section 1 — Organization

- Company name
  - Type: Short answer
  - Required: Yes

- Primary contact name
  - Type: Short answer
  - Required: Yes

- Contact email
  - Type: Short answer
  - Required: Yes

- Company address / city
  - Type: Short answer
  - Required: Yes

---

## Section 2 — Opportunity Details

- Name of opportunity / project
  - Type: Short answer
  - Required: Yes
  - Example: Data Dashboard Sprint, IT Support Internship

- Type of engagement
  - Type: Multiple choice
  - Required: Yes
  - Options:
    - Internship
    - Project-based
    - Short-term role

- Goal of opportunity
  - Type: Paragraph
  - Required: Yes
  - Help text: What should the candidate accomplish or learn?

- Day-to-day candidate activities
  - Type: Paragraph
  - Required: Yes
  - Help text: Describe the typical tasks and activities.

- Technical skills required or desired
  - Type: Short answer
  - Required: Yes
  - Example: Python, Azure, Tableau, Excel, SQL, networking, help desk

- Business knowledge or background desired
  - Type: Short answer
  - Required: No
  - Example: Healthcare, finance, operations, nonprofit

- Duration
  - Type: Short answer
  - Required: Yes
  - Example: 6 weeks, 3 months

- Expected hours per week
  - Type: Short answer
  - Required: Yes
  - Example: 10–15 hours per week

- Work location
  - Type: Dropdown
  - Required: Yes
  - Options:
    - In-person
    - Hybrid
    - Remote

- Project stage
  - Type: Dropdown
  - Required: No
  - Options:
    - New project
    - Ongoing project
    - Exploratory / undefined

---

## Section 3 — Supervision & Environment

- Candidate supervisor
  - Type: Short answer
  - Required: Yes
  - Help text: Name and role of the person supervising the candidate.

- Level of team interaction expected
  - Type: Dropdown
  - Required: No
  - Options:
    - Mostly independent
    - Regular team collaboration
    - High interaction / client-facing

- Laptop / equipment
  - Type: Multiple choice
  - Required: No
  - Options:
    - Candidate brings own
    - We provide one

- Hosted an intern or project resource before?
  - Type: Multiple choice
  - Required: No
  - Options:
    - Yes
    - No, first time

- Workplace culture or environment
  - Type: Short answer
  - Required: No
  - Example: Fast-paced startup, collaborative nonprofit, corporate team

---

## Section 4 — Internal / Matching Notes

- Is compensation available?
  - Type: Dropdown
  - Required: No
  - Options:
    - Yes — paid
    - Unpaid / credit only
    - Open to discussion
    - Seeking Hub financial support

- Possibility of extended or follow-on work?
  - Type: Dropdown
  - Required: No
  - Options:
    - Yes, likely
    - Possible
    - No
    - Unknown

- Preferred visibility on platform
  - Type: Multiple choice
  - Required: No
  - Options:
    - Name company publicly
    - Keep anonymous initially

- Ideal start date or timeframe
  - Type: Short answer
  - Required: No
  - Example: September 2026

- Additional ideal candidate notes
  - Type: Paragraph
  - Required: No
  - Help text: Anything else that would make a candidate ideal?

---

## Confirmation Message

Thank you for submitting an opportunity to The Exchange. The Exchange team will review your submission and follow up if additional information is needed.

# Candidate Interest Form

Form title:
The Exchange — Candidate Interest Form

Form description:
Use this form to express interest in being considered for work-based learning opportunities through The Exchange. Candidates are reviewed for skills, readiness, availability, and alignment with available opportunities before being matched with employers.

---

## Section 1 — About You

- Full name
  - Type: Short answer
  - Required: Yes

- Email address
  - Type: Short answer
  - Required: Yes

- Phone number
  - Type: Short answer
  - Required: No

- Town or city
  - Type: Short answer
  - Required: Yes
  - Example: Stamford, Bridgeport, Norwalk

---

## Section 2 — Education & Training

- Current or most recent school or program
  - Type: Short answer
  - Required: Yes
  - Example: CT State, UConn, Synchrony Skills Academy, DAE

- Field of study or program focus
  - Type: Short answer
  - Required: Yes
  - Example: Cybersecurity, Data Analytics, IT, Software Development

- Enrollment status
  - Type: Dropdown
  - Required: No
  - Options:
    - Currently enrolled
    - Recently completed program
    - Graduate within 2 years
    - Career changer / returning learner

- Expected or actual completion date
  - Type: Short answer
  - Required: No
  - Example: May 2026, already completed

---

## Section 3 — Skills & Interests

- Technical skills developed
  - Type: Short answer
  - Required: Yes
  - Example: Python, SQL, Excel, networking, help desk, cybersecurity basics

- Desired type of work
  - Type: Paragraph
  - Required: Yes
  - Help text: What types of problems, projects, or work environments are you interested in?

- Skills to build through experience
  - Type: Paragraph
  - Required: Yes
  - Help text: What hands-on experience do you want to gain?

- Industries or sectors of interest
  - Type: Short answer
  - Required: No
  - Example: Healthcare IT, finance, government, nonprofit, education

---

## Section 4 — Availability & Logistics

- Available start date
  - Type: Short answer
  - Required: Yes
  - Example: September 2026, immediately

- Hours available per week
  - Type: Dropdown
  - Required: Yes
  - Options:
    - Up to 10 hours/week
    - 10–20 hours/week
    - 20+ hours/week
    - Full-time

- Work location preference
  - Type: Multiple choice
  - Required: No
  - Options:
    - In-person
    - Hybrid
    - Remote
    - Open to any

- Reliable transportation or remote access
  - Type: Multiple choice
  - Required: No
  - Options:
    - Yes
    - Transportation is a barrier
    - Need remote setup support

---

## Section 5 — Supporting Materials

- LinkedIn profile
  - Type: Short answer
  - Required: No
  - Help text: LinkedIn URL

- Resume
  - Type: File upload
  - Required: Yes

- GitHub or portfolio
  - Type: Short answer
  - Required: No
  - Help text: GitHub or portfolio URL

- Additional notes
  - Type: Paragraph
  - Required: No
  - Help text: Share any additional context, goals, barriers, or matching notes.

---

## Confirmation Message

Thank you for expressing interest in The Exchange. The Exchange team will review your information and follow up if there is an opportunity that aligns with your skills, interests, and availability.
