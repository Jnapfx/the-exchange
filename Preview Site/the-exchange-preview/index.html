What is The Exchange?

A coordinated pathway—not another job board.

The Exchange connects employers, students, recent graduates, program completers, and early-career talent through internships, short-term roles, and project-based engagements.

It is not a staffing service or résumé database. It is a structured approach to meaningful, time-bound work experiences that benefit both employers and emerging talent.

Why The Exchange?

Closing the experience gap from both sides.

Early-career talent is trained and ready to contribute, but often lacks access to the applied experience employers expect. At the same time, businesses have real projects and emerging skills needs that can become meaningful learning opportunities.

How It Works

A simple path from interest to applied experience.

  1. 1

    Employers submit opportunities

    Businesses share an internship, project, or short-term role through the intake form.

  2. 2

    Candidates express interest

    Applicants submit skills, training, interests, availability, and supporting materials.

  3. 3

    The Exchange reviews for fit

    The team evaluates readiness, scope, supervision, and alignment on both sides.

  4. 4

    Matching begins

    Aligned candidates are surfaced to employers from a curated, pre-vetted pool.

  5. 5

    Work-based learning begins

    The selected candidate and employer begin a structured, time-bound engagement.

  6. 6

    Outcomes are documented

    Feedback helps improve the model for the next cycle.
